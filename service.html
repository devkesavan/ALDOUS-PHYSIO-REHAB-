The Services We Provide

Premium Restorative Physio

The Physical MOT at Aldous Physio Rehab provides a comprehensive assessment of your physical health within a one-hour session. We evaluate your joints, strength, balance, agility, and cardiovascular fitness to ensure you can exercise without pain and recover swiftly while preventing future injuries. Our goal is to expedite your return to training so you can continue enjoying your sport.

Women’s Health Care

Women’s health is a specialised area within physiotherapy that addresses a wide range issue affecting the pelvic and abdominal region ranging from pregnancy related complications to urinary incontinence. Physiotherapy has been shown to be an effective treatment in pelvic health issues, improving symptoms and womens quality of life. Women should not suffer in silence anymore.
