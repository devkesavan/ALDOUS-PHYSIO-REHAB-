Our story

ALDOUS PHYSIO REHAB

The Good Shepherd Charitable Trust [ Reg No : 284/94], founded in 1994 by Dr. W.C. Thomas, founder and chairman is dedicated to organizing various social initiatives, including medical camps and sports events.
Aldous Physio Rehab operates under the auspices of this trust, and Led by the esteemed physiotherapist T.E. Winnie BPTh..,MPTh [Neurology] IAP-Reg No: 62762 and has received training from esteemed Hospitals such as Apollo Hospital Greame Road, Kauvery Hospital, Government Medical College, Omandurar Government Estate, and Meenakshi Medical College Hospital And Research Institute and her team have endeavoured to maintain their reputation for providing truly individualised patient care with superior levels of customer service.
The majority of patients and clients are referred to the clinic by doctors, surgeons and consultants that are confident their patients will receive the very best care.

"OUR PASSION IS TO HELP YOU GET BACK TO MOVING CONFIDENTLY WITHOUT SURGERIES OR INJECTIONS."

    

Our Expert Team: The Heart of Aldous Physio Rehab

The heart of Aldous physio rehab is our dedicated and skilled team of registered physiotherapists. Each member of our team is not only highly qualified, but also deeply committed to providing the highest level of patient care. Their blend of expertise, empathy, and passion is what sets our clinic apart.
At Aldous physio rehab, we believe that the best physiotherapists are those who go beyond the academic and technical aspects of their role. They are lifelong learners, staying current with the latest research and advances in the field.Our physiotherapists understand the physical, emotional, and psychological impacts of injury and chronic pain. They take a holistic approach to treatment, which means they consider all aspects of your wellbeing, not just the symptoms of your condition. They work closely with you to understand your needs and goals, and they are committed to helping you achieve them.
